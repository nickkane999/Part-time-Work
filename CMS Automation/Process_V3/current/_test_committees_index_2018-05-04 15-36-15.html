Core Curriculum Review Committee

Chair

Annie Farrell (ACC)

Members

  • Bill Even (ECO)
  • Yvette Harman (FIN)
  • Gabe Lee (ISA)
  • Joe Rode (MGT)
  • Gillian Oakenfull (MKT)
  • Janice Kinghorn (Assessment Director)
  • Heidi McKee (Howe Writing Initiative Director, ex officio)
  • Marti Kyger (Assistant Dean for Enrollment Management & Director of Divisional Advising, ex officio)

Dean's Liaison

Barnali Gupta

Committee Charge

With the implementation of the first-year BQ curriculum during the fall semester 2016, this Committee is asked to review the remaining FSB core courses, excluding the FSB capstone courses. Recall, the BQ curriculum is an eight-hour integrated first-year core focused on the skills of Collaboration, Creativity, Computational Thinking and Communication overlaid with a focus on Globalization, Diversity, Technology and Ethics. The BQ core is designed to develop a student's 'Business Quotient (BQ),' and incorporates four distinct two-hour courses, with each course focused on a specific skill. In addition, to introduce first-year students to experiential learning, BQ incorporates a client-based project that incorporates all four skills.

Given first-year students will have experienced the BQ core, this committee is asked to recommend how the remaining FSB core courses might be reimagined to reflect the skills of collaboration, creativity, computational thinking and communication overlaid with a focus on globalization, diversity, technology and ethics.

  1. The Committee will meet to address its annual charge.
  2. Meeting minutes will be taken.
  3. The Committee Chair will provide a summative year-end report of Committee activities to the Associate Dean for Curriculum. This report will be added as a consent item to the agenda of the final spring faculty meeting.
